Company Overview

One intelligence layer for fragmented factory operations.

Flowmatic builds software for brownfield factories where CNC machines, robots, cameras, inspection tools, operators and spreadsheets already exist — but still operate as disconnected islands.

Our platform connects these assets through a shared Event Layer and process-context model. It begins with practical modules such as NC toolpath analysis, camera-based cycle-time tracking and quality-event analysis, then expands toward AMR orchestration and closed-loop process control.

The Problem

Automation exists. Operational intelligence is missing.

Factories already own machines, robots, sensors and software. Yet people still have to collect signals, interpret context, prioritize actions and continuously push the system forward.

Hidden losses

Cycle-time drift, micro-stoppages, tool wear, logistics delay and operator intervention often remain invisible until production loss has accumulated.

Fragmented systems

CNC, PLC, MES, robots, cameras, inspection files and Excel reports do not share one operational language.

Human bottlenecks

Experienced engineers repeatedly connect the dots between process, quality, equipment and logistics through manual judgment.

Modern factories already have eyes, hands and legs. Flowmatic adds the brain and nervous system.
